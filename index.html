How Ecomma Works

From browsing to delivery — here's how shopping on our platform works

  1. 1

    Browse & search

    Explore rooms — living room, kitchen, bedroom, and more — or search for exactly what you need.

  2. 2

    Add to cart or wishlist

    Save favorites for later or add items from multiple sellers into one cart.

  3. 3

    Checkout & pay

    Pay securely with MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, bank card, or cash on delivery.

    Track & receive
